Original story of shinchan

Shinnosuke Nohara (野原しんのすけ), also known as Shinchan or just Shin,
is the titular main protagonist of Crayon Shin-chan. He is the son of Hiroshi and Misae.
His nickname is "Shinchan" and he is Himawari's older brother.
A kindergarten-aged boy whose antics are the basis for the series.
